# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

This project is intended to be a respectful, technically serious open-source space. Participants are expected to communicate directly, assume good intent, and avoid personal attacks, harassment, or exclusionary behavior.

## Expected behavior

- Focus criticism on ideas, code, and decisions rather than people.
- Be respectful in review comments, issues, and discussions.
- Provide context when challenging a proposal or implementation.
- Help keep the project accessible to new contributors.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language
- Doxxing or publication of private information
- Repeated bad-faith disruption of technical discussions
- Abusive behavior in issues, pull requests, discussions, or other project spaces

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that violates this code of conduct and may restrict participation when necessary to protect the project and its contributors.

## Reporting

Report serious conduct issues to the maintainers using the contact path in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). Reports will be reviewed privately and handled as promptly as possible.
